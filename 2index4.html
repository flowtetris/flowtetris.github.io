Example screenshots

There will be four questions (presented in randomised order each time) in which you will rate on a scale of 1-4.

For example, for the first question, 'Where was your attention just before these questions?':

- Please answer '4' if you were attention was focused on the game

- Please answer '3' if your attention was somewhat focused on the game

- Please answer '2' if your attention was not really focused on the game

- Please answer '1' if your attention was not at all focused on the game
